What games am I obsessed with right now?

1. Shadow of the Tombraider: The Nightmare

I have always been a fan of the tombraider franchise. In fact, "Tombraider: Rise of the Tombraider" is one of the games that inspired me to become a video game desginer/developer

I highly recomment playing any of game from the tombraider trilogy -- but be warned: you will not be able to put the game down
